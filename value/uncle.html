Bruce Earl Law, 94, passed away February 24, 2012. Funeral service will be 12Noon, Wednesday, February 29, 2012, at Floral Hills. Interment will follow. Visitation will begin at 11:00am. He was born January 14, 1918, in Springfield, MO, to Earl and Ellen (Ruyle) Law. He was a Kansas City area architect and designed many homes, schools and churches. He was a member of the A.I.A., Ararat Shrine and One Spirit United Methodist Church. He was preceded in death by Joan, his wife of 60 years, in 2002. Survivors include his daughters and their husbands; Susan and Tom Grube and Alice and Jerry Beshoner; sister, Helen (Spiller) Rounds; grandchildren, John Grube, Fr. Seraphim Beshoner, Lisa Eberhardy and Benjamin Beshoner; 11 great grandchildren, and 1great-great granddaughter. In lieu of flowers, memorial tributes may be directed to One Spirit United Methodist Church, 7900 Blue Ridge Blvd., KCMO 64138. Fond memories and condolences for the family may be left at www.floralhillsfuneralhome.com. (Arrangements by D.W. Newcomer's Sons Floral Hills Funeral Home, 816-353- 1218) D. W.
